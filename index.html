About Trending Professionals

Who We Are

Trending Professionals is an MSME- and GST-certified company based in Ghaziabad, founded in 2023. We specialize in providing end-to-end manpower and training support, tailored for national-level operations that require scalable execution and real-time management.

At Trending Professionals, we don’t just provide services we deliver tailored solutions that drive real, measurable results. With a client-first approach, we craft impactful strategies backed by industry expertise and innovation. Our team ensures every project is executed with efficiency, creativity, and precision. Whether you're a startup or an established enterprise, we are committed to timely delivery, consistent support, and outcomes that exceed expectations. Your satisfaction and success are at the core of everything we do.

Our Vision

To contribute towards building a digitally empowered India by organizing and uplifting the skilled workforce fraternity. We envision creating an ecosystem where skilled labour is recognized, empowered, and integrated into the mainstream economy through technology and structured processes — supporting the Government of India’s vision of digitalization and skill development.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between skilled labour, businesses, and end-users by offering a seamless, reliable, and quality-driven service experience. We are committed to empowering skilled professionals, streamlining service delivery, and ensuring that every customer receives hassle-free, timely, and high-quality services — driving both economic growth and social development in a new Digital India.

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Trending Professionals provide?
We offer end-to-end technical services including appliance installation & maintenance (ACs, TVs, chimneys, etc.), background checks and employee verification, security surveillance setup (CCTV, DVR), and comprehensive service management solutions.
How can I request a service from Trending Professionals?
For individual customers (B2C):

You can easily request a service by visiting our official website, browsing through the available service categories (such as appliance installation, CCTV setup, or background verification), and filling out a simple booking form. Once submitted, our support team will promptly reach out to confirm your request, provide pricing details, and schedule the service at your convenience.

For businesses (B2B):

If you represent a brand, enterprise, or organization seeking a scalable service solution, Please directly contact our enterprise support team via email or phone. We provide customized service packages, deployment support, and dedicated account management to ensure smooth onboarding and execution tailored to your operational needs.

Are your service personnel verified and trained?
Yes, all our technicians and support staff undergo strict background checks and skill verification. We also provide training aligned with industry standards to ensure top-quality service.
Do you provide services in rural and remote areas?
Absolutely! We are expanding to cover all 19,101 pin codes across India, including urban, semi-urban, and remote areas through our vast technician network and mobile workforce.
What makes Trending Professionals different from other service providers?
Trending Professionals sets itself apart through trusted collaborations with renowned brands like Panasonic, LG, and Onsite GO, reflecting our credibility and industry alignment. Our transparent, pay-per-service pricing ensures clients always know what they’re paying for—no hidden charges or surprises. We also offer real-time service tracking to keep users informed every step of the way. Above all, our strong focus on customer satisfaction and rigorous quality assurance processes ensures that every service we deliver meets the highest standards.
